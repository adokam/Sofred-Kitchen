Our Story

We remain your best local delicious meal in Abuja

Sofred Kitchen & Bar is Abuja's favourite destination for authentic Nigerian cuisine. From our warm restaurant in Apo Resettlement Park to your event venue across the city, we bring the true taste of home to every plate.

Whether you are craving Egusi Soup and swallow, a cold bottle of our signature Tigernut Milk, or need a full catering spread for 200 guests, Sofred has you covered.

We do delivery within Abuja and cater for weddings, corporate events, birthday parties and naming ceremonies.
