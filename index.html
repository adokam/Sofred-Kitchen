Our Story

Abuja's Favourite
Local Kitchen

We are Abuja's favourite local kitchen — serving authentic Nigerian meals with love, from our restaurant to your event. At Sofred Kitchen & Bar, every plate tells a story rooted in culture, warmth, and bold West African flavours.

From steaming bowls of Egusi Soup served with perfectly pounded yam to tall glasses of our house-made Tigernut Milk, we bring the true taste of home to every meal — whether you dine in, order for delivery, or invite us to your event.
